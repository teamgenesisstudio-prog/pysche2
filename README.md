PSYCHE

### Mental health app that asks, doesn't spy.

---

## 🧠 What is PSYCHE?

PSYCHE is a mental health and self-improvement app that respects your privacy.

It doesn't track your location. Doesn't read your messages. Doesn't sell your data.

It asks how you're doing. That's it.

---

## ✨ Features

### 🏠 Home Screen
- Live PSYCHE Score (0-100) based on your check-ins
- Pattern detection (low mood, poor sleep, isolation, social withdrawal)
- Micro-actions tailored to your patterns
- Weekly stats (mood, sleep, social, check-ins)

### 💬 Chat
- Powered by Claude AI
- Streaming responses (word‑by‑word)
- Mental health support
- Workout blueprints
- Sports training plans (soccer, basketball, arm wrestling, tennis, WWE, cricket, boxing, swimming, running, gym, yoga, esports)
- General conversation
- Crisis detection with local hotlines
- Persistent chat history (stays on your device)

### ✅ Check‑in
- 5 optional questions:
  - How did you sleep?
  - Sleep position?
  - Mood today (1‑10)?
  - Did you leave home?
  - Did you socialize?
- All answers stored locally

### 📓 Journal
- Write entries, delete anytime
- Automatic sentiment detection (positive/negative/neutral)
- View mood trends over time

### 📈 Progress
- 7‑day mood chart
- Check‑in streak counter
- Weekly summary
- PSYCHE insights

### 🏋️ Sports & Workout Blueprints
- Position‑specific plans (soccer: goalkeeper, defender, midfielder, winger, striker; basketball: point guard, shooting guard, small forward, power forward, center)
- Skill levels: beginner → intermediate → advanced → elite
- Drill libraries with 40+ drills per sport
- Weekly training scheduler
- Video tutorial integration (YouTube)
- Progress tracking (personal records, skill ratings)
- Nutrition, recovery, and injury prevention modules

### 🔒 Privacy
- All data stored locally in your browser (localStorage)
- No data sent to servers unless you chat with Claude
- Delete all your data with one tap
- No tracking without permission
